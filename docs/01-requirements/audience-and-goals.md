# Audience and goals

## Primary audiences

1. **Prospective guests** — People evaluating a Haliburton-area cottage rental who need a clear sense of the property, location, and how to book.
2. **Confirmed guests** — People who have booked and want practical guides (arrival, parking, what to bring, cooking, local activities) before and during their stay.
3. **Property owners / editors** — People who maintain copy, images, and structure; they need a predictable content workflow (see `02-solution`).

## Business goals

- **REQ-001:** Support the primary booking path (external platform—e.g. Airbnb) with clear calls-to-action from the site.
- **REQ-002:** Reduce repetitive pre-arrival questions by publishing accurate, findable guest information.
- **REQ-003:** Present the property as a four-season, nature-oriented retreat consistent with the actual experience.

## Non-goals

- Operating a full booking engine or payment flow on this site (handled off-site).
- Multi-language content unless explicitly added later (current intent: English only).
- Public comment threads on posts (comments remain disabled in theme config unless requirements change).
